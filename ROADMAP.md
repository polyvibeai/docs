## Phase 2 â€” Social Engagement Activation

**Goal:** Activate the social layer of the product and prove the public feed creates discovery and virality.

**Prerequisites:** Phase 1 metrics validated. Users are creating avatars, generating looks, and showing retention signals.

**Definition of Success:**
- Community feed has meaningful daily content volume
- Feed-to-follow conversion rate shows users discovering and following others
- Post-to-affiliate-click rate demonstrates commerce intent from social content
- Organic growth observable (new users finding app through community posts)

### Key Features Shipping This Phase
- Public feed (algorithmic + chronological)
- Public profiles with shareable style grids
- Follow / follower mechanics
- Post-to-shop affiliate integration in feed
- Community content moderation pipeline
- Save / wishlist for community-discovered looks
- In-app sharing (share a generation directly to TikTok/Instagram from app)

### Key Milestones
- Feed design and engineering complete
- Affiliate link integration live and attributed
- Content moderation tooling operational
- Referral / invite mechanics built (sharing generates invite to friend)
- User interviews conducted to validate social feature engagement

---

## Phase 3 â€” PRO Subscription Launch

**Goal:** Introduce monetization via PRO tier and validate willingness to pay.

**Prerequisites:** Phase 2 validated. Active social engagement and meaningful returning user base established.

**Definition of Success:**
- 5%+ of active users convert to PRO within 60 days of launch
- PRO churn < 5% per month
- MRR growing month-over-month

### PRO Tier Features
- Unlimited monthly generations (free tier capped)
- Priority generation queue (faster results)
- Advanced customization (multi-item outfits, fine-tuned avatar controls)
- Early access to new categories (interior design, beauty)
- Exclusive PRO badge on public profile (status signal for social users)

### Key Milestones
- PRO paywall design and engineering complete
- App Store in-app purchase integration
- Free tier generation limits implemented
- PRO upgrade prompts placed contextually (not aggressively)
- Pricing validated through user interviews and A/B testing
